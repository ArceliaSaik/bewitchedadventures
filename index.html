I miss the green grass, the tall trees, and the abundance of flowers from Indiana but the Arizona desert has its own unique beauty that I don't really want to go far from. I've fallen in love with my environment and am happy to stay in Arizona where so many biomes are not far from me.

Nature and religion have always inspired and called to me. Together these things have helped me develop a beautiful spirituality and educational background that are very nature centered. My passion in life is to help people and bring myself a sense of peace through helping myself as much as I help others. I feel that it is very important at this point in my life to rediscover myself and figure out how to implement peace in my life through nature, mindfulness, and spirituality.

I am a survivor and I live my life day to day trying to make the best out of what I have. Although it isn't always the easiest thing to remain optimistic, I feel I do a pretty good job most days.

I have loving friends and family that are never far away. I want us all to have fun and be at peace. And I want to inspire those around me to have fun and be at peace in their lives.

So let's laugh and dream together.

~ Spirit
