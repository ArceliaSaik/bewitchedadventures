Scarlett Morrigan Voldaren

Campaign Information: This game is a D&D 5e & Magic The Gathering game. This campaign is run by yours truely and one of my besties, Casper.

Titles: Lady (Innistrad), Officer (Ravnica), Miss Morrigan (Earth 2001)

Quotes: "You don't know music until you hear Elvis and Metallica, Buddy."

Apparent Age: "Late teens/early 20s but is a known vampire so it's anyone's guess how old she is."

Apparent Alignment: Chaotic Sassy

Race/Template: Human (Stencia)/Vampire (Grim Hallow)

Class/Subclass/Level: Witch Knight/TBD/1


Occupation: Azorius Arrestor/Private Investigator; Bed and Breakfast Owner

Personality: Sassy but kind-hearted and open. Often quiet (when uncomfortable, for those that know her) but always calculating.

Ravnica Guild: Azorius Senate

Religion: Sanguine Catholicism (A vampiric Catholic denomination popular on Earth.)

Interests: Playing games (kind of a weakness), keeping order so others can enjoy themselves as well, combat/training, adrenaline inducing activities, generally just doing things that bring her joy and if it brings others joy at the same time... wonderful!

Hobbies: Brewing/cooking blood drinks/foods, singing, dancing, riding horses, piano, gardening/flower arranging.

Other: Wears a citrine and diamond ring and carries a parasol. Always wearing a cross.

Playlist: Scarlett's Playlist

Attire: Scarlett's Clothing Choices

Timeline & Ties: History

About Once Upon a Mystery Bed and Breakfast: The small mansion masquerades as an upscale Bed & Breakfast and acts as supplementary income, Scarlett's home on Ravnica, as well as her P.I. Office when she's not doing official work for the Azorius Senate. It is peaceful and classy establishment with only 9 rooms open to patrons (3 singles, 4 doubles, and 2 triples) and a penthouse for herself. She usually hosts clients at the B&B while she works on cases for them or for teammates who she needs close by. There is a small amphitheater with a stage at the bottom, a sizable lounge with a fully stocked bar and a dance floor flanked by small curtained alcoves equipped with booths, and there are 24/7 butlers and maids serving the establishment. Surrounding the establishment is a small garden packed with fragrant and beautiful flowering plants, as many blooming at night as during the day. The Bed & Breakfast sits in Gate Watch Plaza and was funded by a mysterious white-haired man that no one knows much about. Whoever her patron is it is rumored that she did him a great service to win his favor to that degree.
